hispaniola

haitian creole: ispayola

Hispaniola, the island shared by mordern-day Haiti and the Dominican Republic, is located in the Caribbean Sea between Puerto Rico, Jamaica, and Cuba. It is fewer than 700 miles southeast of Florida. Haiti occupies the western third of the island, and is home to nearly 10 million people. The country is known for its remarkable history and culture: Haita was the first independent nation in the Caribbean, the second democracy in the western hemisphere, and the first black republic in the world.
